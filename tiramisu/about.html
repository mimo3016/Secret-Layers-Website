Serena Mendolia

TIRAMISU HAS ALWAYS BEEN MY LOVE STORY. ❤️

From a young age, back home in Sicily, I found joy in the simplest layers of mascarpone and coffee-soaked savoiardi. I started making tiramisu in my family kitchen when I was just a teenager. People kept asking for it,
and by 15, my mum jokingly said, "You should open a business!"

Well… fast forward (a few) years, and here I am.

After nearly a decade of living in London, I've decided to bring a little Sicilian love to this city I now call home.

This shows if you have a plan in mind and you put the necessary work into it, every dream is achievable even the ones that seem impossible.

With love from Sicily to London
